About Me

The analyst behind the maps

S

Shachi · India

I'm a geospatial and environmental data analyst with a deep interest in how climate change reshapes landscapes, cities, and ecosystems. My work sits at the intersection of earth observation, statistical modelling, and data storytelling.

I use satellite imagery, climate model outputs (CMIP6), and GIS workflows to turn raw raster and vector data into findings that support environmental research and evidence-based decision-making. Whether it's quantifying urban heat intensification in Delhi or projecting monsoon variability across the Western Ghats, I care about making complex spatial patterns legible.

I'm actively looking for roles and collaborations in climate analytics, environmental consulting, and geospatial research.
